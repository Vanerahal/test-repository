  • Global

    We are financing receivables from over 135 countries, including Europe, Americas, Australia, Asia and Middle East

  • Expertise

  • Value

  • Committed Capital

    World-class technology combined with excellent structuring has led to zero loss for any funder of a Demica programme

    Our platform is now connected to over 240 ERP and accounting packages, providing daily invoice level data to our risk systems

  • Accreditations

    Alongside the world’s banks, we are members of AFME and adhere to ISO27001 information security standards
